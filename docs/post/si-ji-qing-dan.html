议论文常用关系词 英文版

  1. 表示因果关系 | Cause and Effect

A. 因为/由于

英文 用法 例句
because 最基础，口语常用 Because of the rapid development, society has changed.
since 较正式，已知事实 Since technology advances rapidly, we must adapt.
as 正式，原因明显 As is known, education is crucial for development.
due to 正式，表示直接原因 Due to mobile internet, information spreads faster.
owing to 正式，书面语 Owing to government policies, the situation improved.
in that 正式解释 Success is possible in that we have support.
inasmuch as 文言性强 Inasmuch as resources are limited, we must be careful.

B. 所以/因此

英文 用法 例句
so 最基础，常用 The policy is important, so we must support it.
therefore 正式，书面语 The facts are clear; therefore, the answer is obvious.
thus 正式，学术文体 Thus, we can conclude that education is vital.
as a result 结果导向 As a result of government support, progress was made.
consequently 正式后果 Consequently, society became more diverse.
for this reason 直接因果 For this reason, we must act now.
accordingly 相应地 Accordingly, measures should be taken immediately.
hence 正式，古风 Hence, the solution is clear to all.
  1. 表示转折关系 | Contrast and Opposition

A. 但是/然而/虽然

英文 用法 例句
but 最常用，口语正式通用 It’s important, but difficult to implement.
however 正式转折，独立使用 However, some disagree with this view.
yet 强烈转折，文学性 The idea is appealing, yet impractical.
nevertheless 正式让步转折 Nevertheless, we must persist in our efforts.
still 依然、仍然 It is challenging; still, we must try.
though 让步转折 Though difficult, it is not impossible.
although 让步转折，正式 Although critics disagree, evidence supports this.
on the other hand 另一方面对比 On the other hand, some argue differently.
in contrast 鲜明对比 In contrast, the old method was ineffective.
conversely 相反地 Conversely, the new approach is efficient.
by contrast 通过对比 By contrast, younger people adapt faster.
while 对比关系 While some succeed, others struggle.
whereas 正式对比 Whereas traditional methods are slow, modern ones are fast.
  1. 表示递进关系 | Progression and Addition

A. 而且/并且/更加

英文 用法 例句
and 最基础，常用 Education is important, and it’s beneficial.
moreover 此外，更重要的是 Moreover, this approach saves resources.
furthermore 进一步，更加 Furthermore, research proves its effectiveness.
besides 除此之外 Besides, the cost is reasonable.
in addition 另外，补充 In addition, many countries have adopted it.
additionally 补充说明 Additionally, the results are impressive.
what’s more 更加，口语 What’s more, it benefits all citizens.
not only…but also 不仅…而且 Not only is it effective, but also affordable.
as well 也、同样 Young people benefit, and so do the elderly as well.
too 也，附加 This method works in other countries, too.
in particular 特别是，尤其 In particular, urban areas see rapid change.
especially 尤其，特别 Especially for young people, this is crucial.
  1. 表示让步关系 | Concession

A. 虽然/尽管

英文 用法 例句
although 正式让步 Although it’s difficult, we must try.
though 口语正式通用 Though expensive, it’s worth the investment.
even though 强调让步 Even though critics object, evidence supports it.
even if 即使条件 Even if we fail, we gain experience.
while 虽然但是 While some disagree, most support this view.
whereas 尽管对比 Whereas old methods are slow, this is fast.
in spite of 尽管（+名词） In spite of challenges, we succeeded.
despite 尽管（+名词） Despite opposition, the plan moved forward.
notwithstanding 尽管（文言） Notwithstanding difficulties, progress was made.
admittedly 诚然、无可否认 Admittedly, the cost is high, but benefits are greater.
it is true that 诚然…是真的 It is true that risks exist, but rewards are substantial.
  1. 表示并列关系 | Coordination

A. 同时/另外

英文 用法 例句
and 最基础 Education develops minds, and it builds character.
both…and 两者都 Both government and citizens must participate.
as well as 也、还有 Teachers, as well as parents, play crucial roles.
(,) and 列举多项 Speed, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness matter.
also 也、同样 This applies to developing countries, also.
  1. 表示对比/排列关系 | Enumeration and Listing

A. 首先/其次/最后

英文 用法 例句

second(ly) 其次 Secondly, social benefits are significant.
third(ly) 第三 Thirdly, environmental advantages exist.
finally 最后 Finally, we must ensure sustainability.
lastly 最后（收尾） Lastly, implementation requires planning.
in the first place 首先，首要 In the first place, evidence is compelling.
on the one hand…on the other hand 一方面…另一方面 On the one hand, benefits are clear; on the other, risks exist.
meanwhile 同时、与此同时 Meanwhile, other countries are advancing.
  1. 表示总结/概括 | Summary and Conclusion

A. 总之/综上所述

英文 用法 例句

on balance 总体而言 On balance, the advantages are significant.

高分议论文关系词搭配

开头段

Due to [事实], society has experienced [变化].
Since [现象], [结论] is evident.
As is known, [共识].

主体段

First/Secondly/Thirdly, [论点1]. Moreover, [论点2].
Furthermore, [论点3]. Therefore/Thus, [总结].

让步段

Although some argue that [反方观点], evidence clearly shows that [正方观点].
Admittedly, [承认点], yet/nevertheless, [驳斥点] is more significant.
While [对方观点] has merit, [自方观点] is ultimately superior.

结论段

In conclusion, considering all factors, we must [行动].
Only in this way can we [目标].
Therefore, it is imperative that [强调].

使用建议：

1.	正式文体 → 优先使用 therefore, moreover, furthermore, however, in conclusion
2.	口语文体 → 可使用 so, and, but, also, for example
3.	学术风格 → 多用 thus, hence, consequently, in addition, notwithstanding
4.	避免重复 → 同一篇文章不要频繁使用同一个关系词，要变换使用
