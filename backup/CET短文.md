## 一、时政类 Politics & Current Affairs

### Passage 1: China’s Development and Reform

Since the Reform and Opening up, China has undergone dramatic transformation. The Belt and Road Initiative has become a cornerstone of international cooperation. Urbanization has accelerated rapidly, while Rural Revitalization programs have been established to address regional disparities. The government has made significant efforts in Poverty Alleviation to improve living standards across the nation. Enhanced Productivity and High-quality Development have become central to Chinese Modernization strategies. Infrastructure projects like High-speed Rail systems have revolutionized transportation, connecting the Yangtze River Delta and Pearl River Delta regions. The Greater Bay Area initiative promotes integration between cities. Free Trade Zones and Pilot Free Trade Zones serve as experimental grounds for economic innovation. Meanwhile, the Silver Economy has emerged as governments recognize the needs of aging societies. Elderly Care and Elderly Care Services have become priorities, supported by robust Social Welfare and Social Security systems.

自改革开放以来，中国经历了翻天覆地的变化。“一带一路”倡议已成为国际合作的基石。城市化进程加速推进，同时乡村振兴项目的建立旨在解决地区差异。政府在扶贫方面投入了巨大努力，以改善全国人民的生活水平。提高生产力和高质量发展已成为中国式现代化战略的核心。高铁系统等基础设施项目革新了交通运输，连接了长江三角洲和珠江三角洲地区。大湾区倡议促进了城市间的融合。自贸区和自贸试验区是经济创新的试验地。同时，银发经济应运而生，因为政府日益认识到老龄化社会的需求。养老和养老服务已成为重点工作，由完善的社会福利和社会保障制度提供支持。

## 二、科技类 Technology & AI

### Passage 2: The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transformed from Simple algorithms to sophisticated systems. Traditional Large Language Models could only answer questions, representing the first generation of AI. However, new AI Agents represent a breakthrough—they can directly assist you with complex tasks. The Smart integration of technology permeates modern devices: Smart phones, Smart cars, and Smart economies. Understanding the fundamental concept of Tokens is essential to grasp how these systems process information. Beyond AI, Cryptocurrency and Blockchain technology have revolutionized the Financial sector, with Blockchain serving as the technological foundation for Bitcoin. The Digital Economy and Digitalization trends have made E-commerce ubiquitous. New technologies include Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), and Augmented Reality (AR), enabling immersive experiences like scanning red envelopes on Alipay. Transportation is being revolutionized by Driverless Cars, Autonomous Vehicles, and Self-driving Cars, supported by Assisted Driving technology. Energy transitions involve Renewable Energy sources: Solar Power, Wind Power, and Hydropower, with Battery technology enabling storage. Data protection requires Cyber Security measures, Privacy safeguards, and Data Security protocols. Meanwhile, the Internet of Things (IoT) connects billions of devices. Big Data and Cloud Computing process vast information streams at unprecedented scale.

人工智能(AI)已从简单的算法演变为复杂的系统。传统的大语言模型只能回答问题，代表了第一代AI。然而，新的AI智能体代表了一个突破——它们可以直接协助你完成复杂任务。技术的智能化融合渗透到现代设备中：智能手机、智能汽车和智能经济。理解令牌的基本概念是掌握这些系统如何处理信息的必要条件。除了AI，虚拟货币和区块链技术革新了金融部门，区块链是比特币的技术基础。数字经济和数字化趋势已使电子商务无处不在。新技术包括虚拟现实(VR)、混合现实(MR)和增强现实(AR)，实现了支付宝上扫红包等沉浸式体验。交通运输正被无人驾驶汽车、自动驾驶汽车和自驾车革新，由辅助驾驶技术支持。能源转变涉及可再生能源来源：太阳能、风能和水电力，电池技术实现了能量存储。数据保护需要网络安全措施、隐私保障和数据安全协议。同时，物联网连接了数十亿台设备。大数据和云计算以前所未有的规模处理海量信息。

## 三、社会文化类 Social & Workplace Culture

### Passage 3: Workplace Equality and Cultural Heritage

Discrimination remains a persistent challenge in many workplaces. Bias and Prejudice often manifest as Stereotypes that perpetuate Clichés about certain groups. Workplace Discrimination based on Sexism and Racism continues to hinder progress toward equality. Despite these challenges, Career Development opportunities should be equally available to all. Some workers choose to Quit or Resign when facing intolerable conditions, leading to Unemployment in some cases. Peer Pressure from colleagues can create unhealthy Work-life Balance issues, but Lifelong Learning and professional growth offer paths forward. Beyond the workplace, society values Traditional and Conventional practices alongside innovation. These Customs represent the intangible culture that defines communities. Cultural Inheritance ensures that knowledge passes to future generations. The Craftsmanship and Exquisite Craftsmanship of artisans produce National Treasures reflecting each Dynasty. Symbols and Symbolism pervade these cultural artifacts, representing Prosperity and Common Prosperity within societies. Ceremonies and Rituals mark important Occasions throughout life.

歧视仍然是许多工作场所面临的持久挑战。偏见和成见经常表现为强化刻板印象的陈词滥调。基于性别主义和种族主义的职场歧视继续阻碍平等进步。尽管存在这些挑战，职业发展机会应该平等提供给所有人。一些员工在面对无法接受的条件时选择辞职，在某些情况下导致失业。来自同事的同辈压力可能会造成工作生活平衡问题，但终身学习和职业成长提供了前进的路径。除工作外，社会既重视传统和常规做法，也重视创新。这些习俗代表了定义社区的非物质文化。文化传承确保知识传递给未来几代人。匠人精神和精湛工艺生产出反映每个朝代的国宝。象征和象征意义遍布这些文化文物，代表社会内的繁荣和共同富裕。仪式和礼仪在生活中标记重要场合。

## 四、校园类 Campus

### Passage 4: University Life and Academic Pursuits

University life begins when Freshmen arrive on Campus, eager to explore Academic opportunities. A Sophomore might suffer more challenges, while Junior Students and Senior Students pursue increasingly specialized studies. Some students continue as Postgraduates, earning a Master’s degree or pursuing a Doctorate and PhD. Most complete an Undergraduate program and earn a Bachelor Degree, the standard credential from a College or University. In the United States, Community Colleges provide accessible Higher Education. The Campus features essential facilities: a Library for research, a Dining Hall for meals, and a Dormitory for student housing. The Campus also includes a Clinic for health services. Academic life revolves around Lectures in large halls—note that university students don’t “have class” in the traditional sense. The Curriculum guides students through required and optional coursework. Extra Curricular activities and Extra Curricular Activities provide enrichment beyond academics. Professors, Associate Professors, and Lecturers teach courses, while Tutors provide individual guidance. Faculty members collectively represent the institution’s academic strength. Students receive Assignments from classes, Handouts with essential information, and opportunities for Internships and Field Work. Field Trips provide practical learning experiences. Modern education includes Online Courses and Distance Learning options, as well as MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) and Telemedicine for health support. Some students attend Cram Schools for test preparation. Academic integrity matters: students must avoid Plagiarism and AI-generated Plagiarism. Finally, scholarships and Tuition support are essential, and students receive Certificates upon program completion.

大学生活从新生到校园开始，渴望探索学术机会。大二学生可能会遭受更多挑战，而大三和大四学生追求越来越专业的学习。有些学生继续作为研究生攻读硕士学位或博士学位。大多数学生完成本科课程并获得学士学位，这是高等院校的标准凭证。在美国，社区大学提供易获得的高等教育。校园配备必要的设施：图书馆用于研究，食堂用于就餐，宿舍用于学生住宿。校园还包括学校医院提供卫生保健服务。学术生活围绕讲座展开——注意大学生在传统意义上不是”上课”。课程大纲指导学生完成必修和选修课程。课外活动和课外活动提供学术之外的充实。教授、副教授和讲师教授课程，导师提供个别指导。教职员工共同代表了机构的学术实力。学生从课程中获得作业、讲义和实习与实地工作的机会。实地考察提供实际学习体验。现代教育包括在线课程、远程教育选项、慕课(大规模开放在线课程)和远程医疗支持。有些学生参加补习班准备考试。学术诚信很重要：学生必须避免抄袭和AI生成的抄袭。最后，奖学金和学费支持至关重要，学生在完成课程后获得证书。

## 五、医疗类 Health & Medicine

### Passage 5: Health, Disease, and Medical Care

Maintaining good Health and Fitness is essential for quality of life. Medical professionals prescribe Medicine in various forms: Pills and Capsules are common delivery methods. Minor Illnesses are manageable, but serious Disease conditions require immediate attention and professional care. Chronic conditions like Diabetes, Heart Disease, and Heart Attack claim millions of lives annually. Stroke, caused by blocked Blood vessels, can result in severe disability. High Blood Pressure and Hypertension are silent killers affecting billions. Obesity, a growing concern worldwide, increases risks for multiple Health conditions. The Immune System provides crucial defense through Immunity mechanisms, fighting against Bacteria, Viruses, and Micro-organisms. Vaccination and Vaccines have been critical public health tools, though Epidemics and Pandemics still pose global threats. Face Masks became ubiquitous during disease outbreaks. Some content goes Viral, spreading across populations like infectious disease. Dementia and mental health conditions like Anxiety and Depression represent growing challenges in aging populations. Symptoms guide Diagnosis, which involves identifying the root cause of Mental State disorders. Infections require treatment, often delivered through Injection methods—shots that deliver medicine directly into the bloodstream.

保持良好的健康和体质对生活质量至关重要。医疗专业人员以各种形式处方药物：药丸和胶囊是常见的给药方式。小病是可控的，但严重疾病需要立即医疗和专业护理。糖尿病、心脏病和心脏病发作等慢性疾病每年夺走数百万人的生命。中风是由血管阻塞引起的，可导致严重残疾。高血压和高血压是影响数十亿人的无声杀手。肥胖是全球日益关注的问题，增加了多种健康状况的风险。免疫系统通过免疫力机制提供关键防御，对抗细菌、病毒和微生物。疫苗接种一直是重要的公共卫生工具，尽管流行病和大流行仍构成全球威胁。口罩在疾病爆发期间无处不在。某些内容会病毒式传播，像传染病一样在人群中传播。痴呆症和焦虑症、抑郁症等心理健康状况代表了老龄化人群面临的挑战。症状指导诊断，诊断涉及识别心理状态障碍的根本原因。感染需要治疗，通常通过注射方法进行——直接将药物注射到血管中的针剂。

## 六、经济发展类 Economic Development

### Passage 6: Economic Systems and Fiscal Policy

Modern economies operate through complex systems of Tax collection and Taxation mechanisms. Governments levy Tariffs on imported goods to protect domestic industries and generate Revenue. Fiscal and Financial policies guide economic management. Nations balance Private Economy initiatives with State-owned Economy enterprises. A strong Business Environment attracts investment and fosters growth. Advanced Manufacturing sectors drive innovation and productivity. Supply Chains and Industrial Chains connect producers to consumers globally, with Chain Shops providing retail distribution. Economic cycles include periods of Inflation, when currency loses value and prices rise, and Deflation, when currency gains strength and prices fall. Government Stimulus packages aim to boost economic activity during downturns, stimulating both consumer spending and business investment.

现代经济通过复杂的税收和征税机制运作。政府对进口商品征收关税，以保护国内产业并产生收入。财政和金融政策指导经济管理。国家平衡民营经济倡议与国有经济企业。强大的营商环境吸引投资并促进增长。先进制造业驱动创新和生产力。供应链和产业链在全球范围内连接生产者和消费者，连锁店提供零售分销。经济周期包括通货膨胀时期，当时货币贬值，物价上升；以及通货紧缩时期，当时货币升值，物价下降。政府经济刺激措施旨在在经济衰退期间刺激经济活动，刺激消费者支出和商业投资。

## 七、环保类 Environmental Protection

### Passage 7: Protecting Our Planet and Environment

Society increasingly embraces Environmentally Friendly practices to combat Pollution and protect the environment through Environmental Protection efforts. Global Warming and Climate Change represent existential threats, with Extreme Weather events becoming more frequent and severe. Natural Disasters like Earthquakes, Floods, and Droughts devastate communities and ecosystems. The Ecosystem supports all life through intricate Ecological relationships. Biodiversity—the variety of bio life forms and diversity of species—faces unprecedented threats. Scientists measure Carbon emissions and emphasize Carbon Neutrality goals to reduce Carbon Dioxide and Greenhouse Gas concentrations. The Greenhouse Effect traps heat, accelerating warming. Sustainable Development balances economic growth with environmental preservation. Deforestation destroys forests, while Afforestation and planting more trees restore them. Recycle initiatives encourage citizens to reduce waste and recover valuable materials from discarded goods.

社会日益采取环境友好型做法来对抗污染，并通过环境保护努力保护环境。全球变暖和气候变化代表了生存危机，极端天气事件变得越来越频繁和严重。地震、洪水和干旱等灾难摧毁社区和生态系统。生态系统通过复杂的生态关系支持所有生命。生物多样性——生物生命形式的多样性——面临前所未有的威胁。科学家测量碳排放并强调碳中和目标，以减少二氧化碳和温室气体浓度。温室效应储存热量，加速变暖。可持续发展平衡经济增长与环保。砍伐森林破坏森林，而植树造林恢复它们。回收倡议鼓励公民减少废物并从丢弃的商品中回收有价值的材料。

Academic writing requires careful organization and structure. A Submission of coursework or a Proposal for a project demonstrates planning and foresight. Job applicants prepare a Resume highlighting qualifications and experience. An Application or formal Apply process involves detailed forms and documentation. 学术写作需要仔细的组织和结构。提交课程作业或提案展示了计划和远见。求职者准备简历突出资格和经验。申请或正式申请流程涉及详细的表格和文件。正式公告和通知通知利益相关者重要决定。新闻报道涵盖当前事件，在如2016年考试等考试中被审视。介绍提供背景信息。总结综合来源材料的关键要点。调查通过问卷和访谈收集数据。广告向目标受众宣传产品和服务。专业信函保持正式语气和结构。每个体裁遵循学术和商业写作人员必须掌握的特定约定。

## 九、科研实验类 Research & Experiments

### Passage 9: Research Methodologies and Experimental Design

Scientific advancement depends on rigorous Research and carefully designed Experiments. Researchers pursue Studies examining specific Phenomena and Projects investigating complex problems. Surveys gather qualitative and quantitative data, while Polls measure public opinion on various topics. The Aim or Objective of research guides the entire investigation. Scientists may state the Purpose, Intention, Target, or Goal—synonymous terms describing desired outcomes. Researchers employ various Practices, Means, Initiatives, Measures, Methods, and Ways to collect data and test hypotheses. Results Show and Demonstrate findings, while data may Indicate or Illustrate patterns, Reveal trends, or Reveal hidden relationships. The Consequence and Result of experiments contribute to broader understanding. Respondents and Participants provide crucial information through surveys and interviews, representing the study population. Effective experimental design ensures that findings are valid, reliable, and reproducible, advancing human knowledge across scientific disciplines.

科学进步取决于严格的研究和精心设计的实验。研究人员开展研究调查特定现象，以及项目调查复杂问题。调查收集定性和定量数据，而民意测验测量大众对各种话题的看法。研究的目标指导整个调查。科学家可能陈述目的、意图、对象或目标——描述所需结果的同义词。研究人员采用各种做法、手段、倡议、措施、方法和方式来收集数据和检验假设。结果显示和证明发现，而数据可能表明或阐明模式、揭示趋势或揭示隐藏的关系。实验的后果和结果促进了更广泛的理解。受访者和参与者通过调查和访谈提供关键信息，代表研究人群。有效的实验设计确保发现是有效、可靠和可重复的，推进各科学学科的人类知识。

## 附录：易混淆词辨析

### Faculty vs. Facility

**Faculty** (教职员工) refers to the academic staff members of an institution.
Example: The Faculty of our university includes renowned scholars and researchers.

**Facility** (设施设备) refers to buildings, equipment, and infrastructure.
Example: The new sports Facility provides excellent training equipment.

**教职员工**是指机构的学术工作人员。
例：我们大学的教职员工包括著名的学者和研究人员。

**设施设备**是指建筑物、设备和基础设施。
例：新体育设施提供优秀的训练设备。

-----

### Artificial vs. Manual

**Artificial** (人造的，特别是AI相关) describes something made or produced by humans or machines, not natural.
Example: Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing industries.

**Manual** (手工的，人工的) describes work done by hand or human labor without machine assistance.
Example: Manual labor requires significant physical effort.

**人造的**（特别是AI相关）描述由人类或机器制造或生产的东西，而非自然的。
例：人工智能正在革新各行业。

**手工的**描述由手工或人力完成的工作，不借助机器。
例：手工劳动需要大量体力。

-----

### Urbanization vs. Modernization

**Urbanization** (城市化) is the process of cities expanding and rural populations moving to urban areas.
Example: Rapid Urbanization has transformed the landscape.

**Modernization** (现代化) is the broader process of adopting modern technology, systems, and values.
Example: Chinese Modernization includes both urban development and technological advancement.

**城市化**是城市扩展和农村人口迁入城市地区的过程。
例：快速城市化改变了风景。

**现代化**是采用现代技术、系统和价值观的更广泛过程。
例：中国式现代化既包括城市发展，也包括技术进步。

-----

### Bachelor vs. Other Degrees

**Bachelor** (学士/学位) refers to an undergraduate degree, typically four years of study. Historically also means an unmarried man.
Example: She earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Biology.

**Master** (硕士/学位) refers to a postgraduate degree requiring one to three years beyond the Bachelor’s.
Example: He pursued a Master’s in Business Administration.

**Doctorate/PhD** (博士) refers to the highest academic degree, requiring extensive research.
Example: Her Doctorate research contributed to medical advances.

**学士**是指本科学位，通常需要四年学习。历史上也指未婚男子。
例：她获得了生物学学士学位。

**硕士**是指本科后的研究生学位，通常需要一到三年。
例：他攻读了工商管理硕士学位。

**博士**是指最高学位，需要广泛研究。
例：她的博士研究为医学进步做出了贡献。

-----

### Inflation vs. Deflation

**Inflation** (通货膨胀) occurs when the general level of prices for goods and services increases, reducing purchasing power.
Example: High Inflation eroded household savings.

**Deflation** (通货紧缩) is the opposite—prices decrease, increasing the value of money but potentially signaling economic weakness.
Example: Deflation can discourage consumer spending and investment.

**通货膨胀**是指商品和服务的总体价格水平上升，降低购买力。
例：高通货膨胀侵蚀了家庭储蓄。

**通货紧缩**是相反的——价格下降，增加货币价值但可能标志经济疲软。
例：通货紧缩可能会阻碍消费者支出和投资。

-----

### Afforestation vs. Deforestation

**Afforestation** (植树造林) is the process of planting trees and establishing forests in areas that were not previously forested.
Example: Large-scale Afforestation projects aim to combat climate change.

**Deforestation** (砍伐森林) is the removal of forests, typically to clear land for agriculture or development.
Example: Rapid Deforestation destroys habitats and increases carbon emissions.

**植树造林**是在以前不是森林的地区种植树木和建立森林的过程。
例：大规模植树造林项目旨在应对气候变化。

**砍伐森林**是去除森林，通常是为了为农业或开发清理土地。
例：快速砍伐森林摧毁栖息地并增加碳排放。
